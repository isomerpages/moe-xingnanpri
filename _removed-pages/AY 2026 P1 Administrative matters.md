<p><strong>Dear Parents/Guardians,</strong>
</p>
<p>We seek your kind cooperation in completing the administrative matters
listed in the table below for your child/ward.
<br>Please note that items marked <strong>“Required”</strong> must be submitted
by <strong>23 October 2025</strong>.</p>
<p>Do keep a lookout, we’ll be sharing <strong>more administrative updates soon</strong> once
the relevant links are available.</p>
<p>We would also like to remind all parents to <strong>sign in to the MOE Parent Gateway (PG)</strong>,
as it will be the <strong>official communication platform</strong> for all
school announcements and updates moving forward.</p>
<p>If you need any clarification, please contact our <strong>General Office</strong> at <strong>6791 3679</strong>.</p>
<p>Thank you for your continued support and partnership.</p>

<p><strong>Parent Gateway Setup (PG)</strong>
</p>
<p>Parents Gateway (PG) is a one-stop mobile app for parents and schools
to better support their children’s educational journey through improved
communications.
<br>
<br>It allows parents to receive updates on school programmes and activities.
It also allows parents to perform administrative functions such as providing
consent for their children to participate in school activities, as well
as declaration of travel plans.
<br>
<br>PG is available on IOS and Android. Open the Apple App Store or Google
Play Store app on your mobile phone to download.&nbsp;</p>
