<h3>Science</h3>
<p><strong>Curriculum Framework</strong>
</p>
<p>The revised MOE Science Curriculum Framework encapsulates the thrust of
Science education in Singapore to provide students with a strong foundation
in Science for life, learning, citizenry, and work.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Science_1.png">
</div>
<p>At Xingnan Primary School, our Science Department is dedicated to nurturing
our students as self-directed inquirers and collaborative innovators. We
seek to inspire a sense of wonder in Science, encouraging Xingnanians to
explore, question, and innovate. Our programmes are designed to provide
authentic and experiential learning opportunities that support the development
of 21st Century Competencies such as collaboration, communication, inventive
and adaptive thinking. We leverage educational technology to personalise
learning experiences and empower students to take ownership of their learning
of Science.</p>
