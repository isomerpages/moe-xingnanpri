<h3>Character and Citizenship Education</h3>
<p></p>
<p><strong><u>Character and Citizenship Education</u></strong>
</p>
<p>We adopt a whole-school approach to developing our students’ character,
resilience, social-emotional well-being, future readiness, and active citizenship
through the various subjects and key student learning experiences (refer
to table below) aligned with Character and Citizenship Education (CCE)
outcomes. Within these subjects and experiences, students are provided
with opportunities to reinforce and apply their learning, with dedicated
time set aside for reflection and personal application.</p>
<p>Beyond instruction, we aspire for all our students to internalise these
principles, qualities, and traits, and to demonstrate positive transformation
from the inside out.</p>
