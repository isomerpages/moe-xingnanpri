### 2022 Teaching Awards

Dear Parent / Student  
  
The 2022 teaching awards for English, Mother tongue Malay and Tamil are now out. you can now nominate your teachers using the links below  
  

_**Inspiring English Teacher of English Award**_   

Nominations for the annual Inspiring Teacher of English Award are now open.  Presented by the Speak Good English Movement (SGEM) and The Straits Times with the support of the Ministry of Education, the Award has two categories – the Teaching Award and the Leadership Award.  

  

Nomination forms for the Teaching Award can be downloaded from http://goodenglish.org.sg/inspiring-teacher-of-english-award/nomination-information.  The Award is open to all in-service teachers of the English Language, including teachers of Literature in English and General Paper.  All students, their parents, and teachers are invited to send in nominations for the Teaching Award to the Principal of the nominee’s school by 6 p.m. on 28 March 2022.

**_Arif Budiman_ Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB)**  

The _Arif Budiman_ Malay Language Teacher Award (AGAB) is an annual award that recognizes outstanding Malay Language teachers who have made significant contributions to the teaching and learning of Malay language in our schools. Since its inauguration in 2007, AGAB has recognized a total of 69 outstanding primaries, secondary and pre-university Malay Language teachers. 

Download the following PDF to read more about the AGAB award selection.'

[Nomination Form AGAB 2022 EL.pdf](/files/Nomination%20Form%20AGAB%202022%20EL.pdf)

[Nomination Form AGAB 2022 ML.pdf](/files/Nomination%20Form%20AGAB%202022%20ML.pdf)
Click on the following link to submit your nomination before the 1st of April 2022: [Go.gov.sg](https://go.gov.sg/agab2022)

#### Most Inspiring Tamil Teacher Award (MIIT) 2022.

_Most Inspiring Tamil Teacher Award (MIIT) 2022._

The _Most Inspiring Tamil Teacher Award (MITT)_ inaugurated in 2002 is an annual award that seeks to recognize Tamil language teachers who have made significant contributions to the teaching and learning of the Tamil language in schools.

You can read more about the MIIT award by downloading the following PDF. To submit your nomination, fill in the attached form given in the pdf and mail it to the Tamil Murusu TLLPC Secretariat based on the given address by 29th April 2022.

[MITT\_Nomination form in EL\_ 2022.pdf](/files/MITT_Nomination%20form%20in%20EL_%202022.pdf)

[MITT\_Nomination form in TL\_ 2022.pdf](/files/MITT_Nomination%20form%20in%20TL.pdf)

  

Alternatively, you can also click on the following link to submit your nomination online: [Mitt 2022 form el](https://go.gov.sg/mitt-2022-form-el) or [Mitt tl 2022 tl](https://go.gov.sg/mitt-tl-2022-tl)
