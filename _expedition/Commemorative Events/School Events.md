<div>

<p><b> Recognition Day 2020 </b> <Br><br> In Xingnan, recognising the achievements of our pupils and staff is of paramount importance.  Recognition Day is a day for us to recognise our pupils for their holistic achievements and our stakeholders for their contributions towards our pupils' successes. The theme of our Recognition Day 2020 is Appreciation in the Face of Adversity. We rose to the occasion and came together as one big family to celebrate the successes of Xingnanians.
 <br><br>
This year's celebration was indeed unique as we celebrated our Recognition Day at different locations in our school. We were able to get the parents to be part of the day's event as it was simultaneously recorded and broadcast in real-time through an online platform.
